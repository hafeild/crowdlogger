Dropping out of the study

As indicated in the informed consent form, you may withdraw from the study at any time for any reason. The easiest way to do this is to uninstall the extension—see the uninstall page.

Before you withdraw, however, take a minute to consider if you want to withdraw or just pause the logging software for some amount of time. You have the ability to pause the logger, which will prevent it from logging data and notifying you of available experiments. For more information about pausing, see the the "Pausing the Logger" section of the help page.
