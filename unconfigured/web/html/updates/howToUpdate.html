How to update your Privacy-Preserving Search Log extension for Firefox

Go to "Tools"->"Add-ons". Select the "Extensions" button or tab at the top. You should see a list of all the extensions you have installed, one of which will be "Privacy Preserving Search Log Toolbar". At the botton of the window, click the "Find Updates" button. There should be an update for the Privacy-Preserving Search Log Toolbar. Click the "Install available updates" button. This will download and install the updates, after which you will be asked to restart your browser.
